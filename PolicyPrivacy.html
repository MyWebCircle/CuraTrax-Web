Introduction

CuraTrax ("CuraTrax," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of users who access our platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (www.curatrax.com) or use any of our services (the "Platform" or "Service"). CuraTrax is owned and operated by Stellite Works, LLC, a limited liability company. By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Platform and Service Definitions

CuraTrax is a healthcare compliance and workflow management platform developed and operated by Stellite Works, LLC. Our services include, but are not limited to, policy management, incident reporting, and credentialing systems. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Service, including clients, administrators, and end-users accessing the Platform through our website, social media channels, or any affiliated websites.

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information:

- Name, email address, telephone number
- Job title, organization name

b. Usage Data:

- Browser type, IP address, access times
- Pages viewed, actions taken on the Platform

c. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

- Session cookies for authentication and navigation
- Analytics cookies to understand user interaction

d. Information Collected via Demo Request Forms:

When you fill out our "Request a Live Demo" form, we collect the following personal and professional information to schedule and tailor your demo experience:

- First and Last Name

This information is collected solely for the purpose of contacting you regarding your demo request, customizing the demonstration based on your business needs, and following up with relevant communications. Your data is handled securely and will not be sold or shared with third parties except as necessary to fulfill the demo request or comply with legal obligations.

We use collected information for the following purposes:
